Our Story

A community built on trust, carried forward by service.

Kachchi Memon Jamat Ner has stood at the heart of Ner's Muslim community for over seven decades — a trust founded on the simple idea that no member should face hardship, education, or opportunity alone. What began as a small gathering of traders and families has grown into a full welfare institution spanning marriage, medicine, education, and enterprise.

President's Message

Kachchi Memon Jamat Ner has always stood for unity, service, compassion, and progress. Our community has grown stronger by preserving our traditions while embracing positive change. Every member plays an important role in strengthening our society. Through education, social welfare, business support, and charitable initiatives, we strive to create a brighter future for the coming generations. May Allah (SWT) continue to bless our community with unity, prosperity, and the ability to serve humanity with sincerity.

Haji Musa Tikki
President, Kachchi Memon Jamat Ner
